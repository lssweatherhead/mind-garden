# The Zettelkasten Method for taking notes

## Important traits for Zettelkasten notes:

- Hypertextual: texts that refer to and explain each other. Emphasis on forming relationships - connecting is the priority not collecting
- Principle of atomicity: each note contains a single unit of knowledge
- it is personal thinking tool

More information here: [Zettelkasten Method](https://zettelkasten.de/introduction/)

# Tectum of midbrain

The tectum of midbrain is a region of the brain, specifically the [[dorsal]] part of the [[mesencephalon]]. This is contrasted with the [[tegmentum]], which refers to the region ventral to the ventricular system. The tectum of midbrain is responsible for auditory and visual reflexes.
